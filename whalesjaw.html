Our Whale's Jaw Tour

This tour begins at Hospital Hill (top of Summit Ave.), is about 3 miles long, and takes approx. 2.5 hours. The tour will take you back in time, as you see:

  • The infamous rock formation Whales Jaw.


  • Walk the boardwalk through Briar's Swamp.


  • See examples of rock formations created by glaciers 10,000 years ago.

The Whale's Jaw tour is a moderate hike recommended for those in good health due to the rocky terrain and hills.*


Tour times vary, please call or email for information. During hunting season (mid to late Fall), tours are available on Sunday, or by special arrangement.

During hikes we break for a surprise historic snack. Complimentary bug spray is provided.

What to bring: camera, water, snack

What to wear: sneakers or hiking boots, hat, jacket and pants
